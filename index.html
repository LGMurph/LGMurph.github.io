I am a graduate student at Purdue University. I am investigating the rapid follow-up of early supernovae warnings from the SNEWS network, developing an off-campus observatory, and teaching introductory astronomy labs.

Current Projects

SNEWS

The SuperNova Early Warning System (SNEWS) is a network of neutrion detectors who send an alert to astronomers when a nearby supernova emits the precursory neutrino burst. I am investigating how to best corrdinate the follow-up effort to find the nearby supernova from the localization provided by the neutrino detectors.

PWAO

The Purdue Wildlife Area Observatory (PWAO) is an off-campus astronomical observatory. I am installing a 14' Schmidt-Cassegrain telescope.
